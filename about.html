FABIAN PITZER – PROGRAMMING DESIGN Enthusiastic about original ideas in the field of digital media, my interdisciplinary skills allow me to deliberate the entire process of materializing them. I am especially intrigued by coding custom design tools with capabilities beyond those of established software. My approach to design is to reduce to the essential, while retaining playfulness. I studied Interactive Media at the UAS Augsburg and the KABK Den Haag and was awarded a scholarship by the German Academic Scholarship Foundation. Following an Internship at Studio Moniker, I worked as a Creative Technologist for Lab Binaer and am now pursuing a master's degree in Visual Communication at the ZHdK in Zürich.
